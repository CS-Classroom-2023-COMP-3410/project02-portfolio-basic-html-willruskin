Top 5 Batman Actors

A ranking of the best Batman actors across movies, comics, and games.


  1. Christian Bale

    Best Batman Trilogy Ever (2005-2012): Christian Bale is fire in all 3 movies. He grows with the character from his origins to his end. Great stuff. Numba 1.


  2. Robert Pattinson

    Robert Pattinson is all time in Matt Reeves' The Batman (2022). 3 hours of genius from start to finish. Batman is hella cool in this film and Robert does an outstanding job.


  3. Kevin Conroy

    Timeless life for some of the most iconic Batman portrayals in animation. Legend of the game.


  4. Michael Keaton

    Batman 1989 is a an absolute classic. Batman Returns is also pretty good too. Michael Keaton is just a legend.


  5. Adam West

    The one and only Adam West. The original Batman.
