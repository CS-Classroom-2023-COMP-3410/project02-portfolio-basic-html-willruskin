Why Batman?

Batman is one of the most iconic superheroes in popular culture. Created by Bob Kane and Bill Finger, Batman first appeared in Detective Comics #27 in 1939. Unlike many superheroes, Batman has no superpowers; instead, he relies on his intellect, detective skills, physical prowess, and an array of high-tech gadgets to fight crime in Gotham City.
